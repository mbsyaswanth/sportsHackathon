About

The Global Innovation Index (GII) has ranked India as the 57th most innovative nation in the world. To improve this index, it is required that better platforms are built and innovation process is standardised. For this to happen, collaboration among Government, Policy Makers, Tech Companies and Academia is required.

It is understood that home-grown digital products are required to benefit local societies and add to the cumulative National economic growth. Sports Authority of Andhra Pradesh and Andhra University are conducting SportTech Challenge, a 36 hour Hackathon to seek the best innovative ideas that would deliver disruptive solutions for the themes focusing on sports infrastructure management, audience engagement and experience enhancement, player/team performance and analytics, solutions for smart stadiums sports equipment technologies IOT and wearable equipments, crowd management and use of biometrics etc.

SportTech Challenge (Hackathon) will bring much required collaboration between the startups and SAAP to bring in the radical changes in the Government processes, outlook and outreach while creating a market for the startups. By finding a solution to a problem in the sports arena, the startups or individuals get an advantage to drive their innovations into the market.

Program Themes

E-SportGov

Products or services for Government for dash board and analytics for the use of resources

Smart Studio

Software/ Software architecture for digital studio –highlighting achievements in sports including simulation techniques and virtual reality.

Smart stadiums

AR/VR/MR solutions, record data for analytics, create data sets for future challenges on events, performances of players, ground conditions, smart advertising to improve income of stadiums, Audience engagement and experience.

Biometric Age

Biometric based technologies to provide services and support to sports personnel, SAAP administration, Play ground/ stadium monitoring system during conduct of events, tournaments, sporting activities etc.

Crowd Management

Crowd Management system

Facilities

Sports health (Physio) centres for sports sciences.

Smart SportsTech

Use of GPS/ sensors or other technologies to observe and record data and use the data for decision making and monitoring. IOT/ sensors for sports fitness of sports personnel to help analyse and give feed back to improve their performance and efficiency. IOT based payment concepts and solutions that can change the world.

Registration

Click here to register your team or directly register below. You will be mailed an id after successfull registration. Every participant shall confirm registration by paying 100 rupees only to the account specified in the mail id. The participant will be provided with food, beverages during the duration of the hackathon. Outstation candidates will be provided free accommodation in AU campus on request.

F.A.Q

  • WILL ALL THE PARTICIPANTS BE GETTING ANY PARTICIPATING CERTIFICATES?

    Yes all the participants, irrespective of the final result of the hackathon will be getting the participation certificates.

  • WHAT IS THE PROCEDURE TO APPLY FOR THE HACKATHON?

    In the website, www.apsport.tech, the registration form is to be filled. Soon after the form is filled, the responses will be reaching the organizers and there will be a confirmation mail sent to the each participant who has filled the form along with their unique ID’s and also a payment information.

    Soon after clicking on the link, you will be redirected to the payment options, after payment of the registration fees, the participants need to send their transaction ID’s to the mail from which they had received the confirmation mails. This completes the registration process.

    The participants who have completed this procedure can directly report at the venue on the day of the hackathon. Preferred time of reporting is 9:00 AM IST.

  • WHAT IS THE FEE STRUCTURE FOR THE HACKATHON?

    Fee per participant is 100/- only and only 3 people per team are allowed (3 people Including the team head). There is no fee for the mentors but more than 2 mentors are not allowed per team. Mentor details are also needed to be filled in the form during registration. There is no fee for the accommodation.

  • DO ALL THE TEAM MEMBERS SHOULD BELONG TO THE SAME INSTITUTION?

    No, all the team members need not belong to the same institution.

  • WHAT IS THE REPORTING TIME OF THE EVENT?

    The reporting time is 9:00 AM IST. The venue is at P.S.N Raju lab, First floor, Principal Block, Andhra University college of Engineering(A).
